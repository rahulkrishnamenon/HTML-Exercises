Govt to conduct sensus of elderly in state

The government will conduct census for elderly citizens in state said chielf minister pinarayi vijayan

While reviewing various projects for elderly citizens by various departments here, the chief minister said the census will be held on the lines of census of physically challenged conducted in 2015. He said the data of functioning old age home and destitute will also be collected. The review committee headed by chielf minister will be reviewed every 6 months
